Who We Are

Tip Toe Soles is a vibrant, family-run shoe company blending tradition with innovation in every step. Known for their signature flexible, noise-dampening soles and understated elegance, they cater to a global clientele who value both comfort and style. The business remains rooted in its artisanal heritage, with each pair reflecting a commitment to sustainable materials and meticulous craftsmanship. Now led by the third generation of the Moretti family, Tip Toe Soles continues to launch limited-edition collections inspired by music, movement, and urban life, while maintaining a loyal community of artists, dancers, and everyday dreamers who walk their own path—quietly, confidently, and always a step ahead.

Our History

Tip Toe Soles began in 1972 in the bustling suburb of Carlton, Melbourne, when shoemaker and jazz enthusiast Leo Moretti noticed a peculiar problem among local musicians: their shoes were too stiff for late-night gigs, making it hard for them to “tiptoe” quietly off stage or tap along to the rhythm. Inspired by the improvisational spirit of jazz, Leo set out to create a shoe that was both flexible and stylish, allowing wearers to move with ease and grace—whether on stage or on the street.

Our Legacy

Today, Tip Toe Soles is still family-owned and operated, with Leo’s grandchildren now involved in design and marketing. The company remains committed to its founding principles: shoes that let you move freely, crafted with care, and designed to let you “step softly, dream boldly.” Each pair carries a discreet embossed note on the insole: “For those who walk their own path, one quiet step at a time.”
